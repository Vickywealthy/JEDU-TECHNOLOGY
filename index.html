ABOUT US

JEDU TECHNOLOGY SERVICES LIMITED is a tech solution company that offers quality, reliable, and affordable products and services to individuals and businesses.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to create an enabling environment that can foster growth and development of people and businesses by replicating global standards in products and services.

Services

Training Packages

We train individuals and corporate organisations on digital and technological skills necessary to increase productivity, increase profit, reduce overhead costs, and promote development.

Services

Computer/Server Maintenance

We equip and maintain computers and physical servers at business premises. We set up and keep the servers running by offering a routine maintenance.

Services

Laptop Sales & Repairs

We sell a variety of laptops shipped directly from global manufacturing brands. Our products are authentic with at least a year warranty. We also offer professional repairs on damaged computer parts.

Services

Security Management

We offer the installation of CCTV and other security gadgets in homes and business premises. We also recover lost data, conduct security and threat analysis on computer and operating systems.

Services

Networking

We set up computer networks - a group of computers that use a set of common communication protocols over digital interconnections for the purpose of sharing resources located on or provided by the network nodes.

Services

Web Development & Hosting

We build and maintain custom- made websites for individuals and businesses.
